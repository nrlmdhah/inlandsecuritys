About Us

Inland Security Services Sdn. Bhd. has been providing trusted security services since 2002. Licensed by the Ministry of Home Affairs and a member of the Malaysian Security Association (PIKM), we ensure top-quality protection for businesses and establishments. With ISO-certified standards, trained personnel, and advanced security systems, we are committed to keeping your premises safe. Headquartered in Batu Caves, Selangor, we have branches across Malaysia to serve you better.
